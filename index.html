Pig Dice

Let's Play Pig Dice

Pig Dice is a fun game. For this particular dice game, you can play two people, with each taking turns and using the same button to roll the decice.

Rules

  • Click on the "Roll" button.
  • Once the dice rolls and gives the first player results, he/she passes it to the second player.
  • The players will keep on alternating the dice between them.
